# Think: Feynman

<command_purpose>Test real understanding by asking the user to explain concepts in their own words. Based on Feynman's principle — if you can't explain it simply, you don't really understand it.</command_purpose>

## Target

<topic> #$ARGUMENTS </topic>

## Instructions

1. Load and follow the **feynman-test** skill (loaded from workspace skills)
2. Ask the user to explain the topic in their own words
3. Evaluate their explanation for clarity, completeness, and genuine understanding
4. Identify gaps and suggest areas to deepen
